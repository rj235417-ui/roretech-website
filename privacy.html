1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Security SPY ("the app"), developed by RORE Tech, handles information on your device. Security SPY is a personal device security application for Android that silently captures photographs when user-configured applications are opened, helping device owners identify unauthorized use.

This policy applies to all versions of the Security SPY app available on the Google Play Store under the package name com.rore.securityspy.

3. How the App Uses Your Camera

Security SPY uses the device's front-facing camera exclusively to capture photographs when a user-configured application is opened or when the device lock screen is accessed. This is the core security function of the app.

Camera access is initiated only when a monitored event occurs. The app does not record video, does not stream to any external service, and does not access the camera at any other time.

All photographs are stored immediately in an encrypted, biometric-protected vault on your device. No photograph is ever transmitted off the device under any circumstances.

4. Biometric Data

During owner registration, the app processes a photograph of the device owner's face using Google ML Kit, which runs entirely on-device. The app computes a set of numerical facial geometry ratios (not a photograph or raw biometric template) and stores only those ratios in your device's local storage.

The photograph taken during registration is processed in memory and is never stored in raw form. Raw biometric data is never transmitted to RORE Tech, Google, or any third party.

The stored geometry ratios are used only to determine whether to skip a capture when the owner opens a monitored app. They cannot be reverse-engineered into a photograph.

5. Accessibility Service

The app uses Android's Accessibility Service API (BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE) to detect when a user-configured application is brought to the foreground. This is the only Android API that provides this capability.

The Accessibility Service used by Security SPY does not read screen content, does not interact with other apps, does not capture keystrokes or input, and does not transmit any information. It is used solely to detect app foreground events for the purpose of triggering the security capture function.

7. Subscription Billing and Payments

Security SPY offers an optional Pro subscription. All subscription purchases, renewals, refunds, and cancellations are processed and managed exclusively by Google Play Billing.

RORE Tech does not collect, store, process, or have access to your payment information. Credit card details, billing addresses, and transaction records are handled solely by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

To manage or cancel your subscription, visit Google Play Store → Menu → Subscriptions on your Android device, or visit play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions.

The app receives only an anonymous entitlement signal from Google Play indicating whether your subscription is active. This signal contains no personal information.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by Security SPY — including photographs in the vault, the event log, and owner geometry ratios — is stored exclusively on your device and remains entirely under your control.

RORE Tech has no ability to access, retrieve, or delete data stored on your device, as we do not have any server-side copy of your data.

How to delete your data

To delete all data stored by Security SPY, use either of the following methods:

Option 1 — Clear app data:
Android Settings → Apps → Security SPY - Silent Lens → Storage → Clear Data
This deletes all photographs, the event log, owner geometry ratios, and your PIN.

Option 2 — Uninstall the app:
Long-press the Security SPY icon → Uninstall
This removes the app and all associated data permanently.

Data types deleted: All captured photographs, facial geometry ratios, app event log, PIN, and app settings. No data is retained after deletion as RORE Tech holds no server-side copy.

For deletion assistance, contact us at privacy@roretech.com

9. Children's Privacy

Security SPY is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all data is stored locally on the device and we collect no personal information whatsoever, this app poses no known privacy risk to minors.
