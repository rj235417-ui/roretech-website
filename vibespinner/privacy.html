Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026  ·  Developer: RORE Tech

The Short Version

We collect nothing personal. Vibe Spinner requires no account, collects no personal data, and transmits nothing off your device. Your gameplay data stays on your phone. That's it.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Vibe Spinner, developed by RORE Tech, handles information in connection with the Application. Vibe Spinner is a mobile entertainment app — an engine spinner simulator with physics-based gameplay, audio synthesis, and visual effects.

Contact: support@roretech.com | roretech.com

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The following information is never collected, stored remotely, or accessible to RORE Tech:

  • Your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Your location or device location
  • Your gameplay statistics or session history (beyond what is stored locally on your device)
  • Your device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID)
  • Any photos you upload to the app (stored locally only)
  • Any financial information beyond what is processed by the app store at purchase

4. In-App Purchases

When you make an in-app purchase (Pro or MAX tier), the transaction is processed entirely by the platform store — Google Play on Android, Apple App Store on iOS. RORE Tech receives confirmation that a purchase was made but does not receive your payment information, card details, or billing address. Purchase records are held by the respective platform store under their privacy policies.

5. Photo Upload Feature (Pro)

Pro and MAX users can upload a personal photo as a spinner background. This photo is stored exclusively in the app's local storage on your device. It is never transmitted to RORE Tech or any third party. You can delete it at any time by clearing app data or changing the background within the app.

7. No Accounts Required

Vibe Spinner does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to play. Download and play — that's it.

8. Data Deletion

All app data is stored on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the Application or clear app data through your device settings. RORE Tech holds no server-side copy of your data and cannot retrieve it.

9. Children's Privacy

Vibe Spinner is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because no personal data is collected by the Application, the app poses no known privacy risk to minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
