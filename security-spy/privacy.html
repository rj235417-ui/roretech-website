Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026  ·  App: com.rore.securityspy  ·  Developer: RORE Tech

The Short Version

Security SPY stores everything on your device. No photos, biometric data, or logs are ever transmitted to RORE Tech or any third party. We cannot see your vault. We have no servers holding your data. This is architectural — not just a policy.

1. About This Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Security SPY (also marketed as "Smart Lens"), developed by RORE Tech, handles information on your device. Security SPY is a personal device security application for Android that silently captures photographs when user-configured applications are opened, helping device owners identify unauthorized access.

This policy applies to all versions of Security SPY available under the package name com.rore.securityspy.

3. How the App Uses Your Camera

Security SPY uses the front-facing camera exclusively to capture photographs when a user-configured application is opened or the device lock screen is accessed. Camera access is initiated only when a monitored event occurs. The app does not record video, does not stream to any external service, and does not access the camera at any other time.

All photographs are stored immediately in an encrypted, biometric-protected vault on your device. No photograph is ever transmitted off the device under any circumstances.

4. Biometric Data

During owner registration, the app processes a photograph of the device owner's face using Google ML Kit, which runs entirely on-device. The app computes numerical facial geometry ratios (not a photograph or raw biometric template) and stores only those ratios in local device storage.

The photograph taken during registration is processed in memory and never stored in raw form. Raw biometric data is never transmitted to RORE Tech, Google, or any third party. The stored ratios cannot be reverse-engineered into a photograph.

5. Accessibility Service

The app uses Android's Accessibility Service API to detect when a user-configured application is brought to the foreground. This is the only Android API that provides this capability.

The Accessibility Service used by Security SPY does not read screen content, does not interact with other apps, does not capture keystrokes or input, and does not transmit any information. It is used solely to detect app foreground events for the purpose of triggering the security capture function.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

All data stored by Security SPY is stored exclusively on your device and remains entirely under your control. RORE Tech has no ability to access, retrieve, or delete data stored on your device.

To delete all data: Android Settings → Apps → Security SPY → Storage → Clear Data. This deletes all photographs, the event log, owner geometry ratios, and your PIN. Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all associated data permanently.

For deletion assistance, contact privacy@roretech.com

8. Children's Privacy

Security SPY is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because all data is stored locally and we collect no personal information, this app poses no known privacy risk to minors.

9. Your Rights

Because we do not collect your personal data, most data subject rights are exercised directly by you within the app. If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the GDPR. Contact us at privacy@roretech.com for GDPR-related requests.
