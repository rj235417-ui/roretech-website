How it works

Catch every intruder.
Miss no false alarms.

🎯
Per-App Monitoring

Tag any installed app. The moment someone opens a monitored app, Security SPY captures a silent photo — no flash, no sound, no warning.

🧬
Owner Face Recognition

Register your face once. When you open your own tagged apps, the capture is skipped automatically — zero false positives, zero friction.

🔐
Encrypted Vault

Every captured photo is stored immediately in a biometric-protected, encrypted vault on your device. No photo ever leaves your phone.

📋
Full Event Log

Every access attempt is timestamped and logged. Pro users get the full history with PDF export — perfect as evidence if needed.

🔇
Completely Silent

No notifications, no UI changes, no sounds. The intruder has no idea they've been photographed. Your security is invisible.

☁️
Zero Cloud

No accounts required. No data transmitted. RORE Tech cannot see your vault, your logs, or your captures. Architecturally impossible.
